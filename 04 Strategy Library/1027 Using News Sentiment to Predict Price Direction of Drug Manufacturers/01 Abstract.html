Several studies have found that press releases and other media can impact the perspective of investors. In this tutorial, we implement an intraday strategy to capitalize on the upward drift in the stock prices of drug manufacturers following positive news releases. Our findings show that when combining the effect with the day-of-the-week anomaly documented by Berument & Kiymaz (2001), there is enough directional accuracy for the trading system to remain profitable throughout the 2020 stock market crash. The algorithm we design here is inspired by the work of Isah, Shah, & Zulkernine (2018).
